Wuu Trade is an artificial intelligence powered financial markets simulation that allows us to trade on historical charts of financial instruments. Compete with fellow traders, long - short the market, get your balance above others, win rewards and improve your skills.

There are demo accounts to practice, although these demo accounts run on live charts, and the wait could be too long and stressful. Wuu Trade, on the other hand, gives you the opportunity to trade on a 1-year chart data even within a 3 minute match. Being extremely fun, you can learn the market faster, test and improve yourself faster.

WuuTrade is an essential tool for a newcomer or a professional trader. Amateurs will use it to improve themselves and learn the market, while professionals will use WuuTrade to test themselves and their strategies, improve their knowledge and skills.

You will earn Wuu NFTs as WuuDrop rewards from us, together with the Gems you collect in the matches you will play in WuuTrade. Burn your WuuNFT and get an instant reward, hold it and combine with other items to craft a super WuuNFT and get a super reward. There are several WuuNFT types, each having its own value and reward. For more information, see our Whitepaper.
